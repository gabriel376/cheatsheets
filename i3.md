# i3 Cheat Sheet

## Session
* Lock: `i3lock`
* Exit: `$mod shift e`
* Restart: `$mod shift r`
* Reload configuration file: `$mod shift c`

## Applications
* terminal: `$mod [return]`
* dmenu: `$mod d`

## Windows
* Switch: `$mod [left down up right]` or `$mod [j k l ;]`
* Move: `$mod shift [left down up right]` or `$mod shift [j k l ;]`
* Close: `$mod shift q`
* Fullscreen: `$mod f`
* Resize: `$mod r`
* Float: `$mod [space]`

## Layout
* Default layout: `$mod e`
* Stacking layout: `$mod s`
* Tabbed layout: `$mod w`

## Workspaces
* Switch: `$mod [0-9]`
* Move window: `$mod shift [0-9]`
